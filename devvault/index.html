Business is NAS-first. Business protection requires a customer-managed NAS or network share. DevVault does not operate customer backup storage, and Business backup is blocked until a valid network vault target is configured. If you are unsure whether your environment is ready, talk to sales before checkout.
What happens after checkout: Checkout creates your annual DevVault subscription. After payment, DevVault sends a signed .dvlic license to the same email you used at checkout, attached at the bottom of that email. If you purchase Business, have your NAS or network share ready before install, because Business backup will not run until an admin configures a network vault. Existing customers can manage billing from the customer billing portal.

License Delivery

What buyers receive after checkout, and what DevVault requires before protected actions can begin.

1. Stripe CheckoutYour purchase starts an annual DevVault subscription for the plan you selected.
2. Signed license issuanceDevVault generates a signed .dvlic license for that purchase and records the license server-side.
3. Email deliveryYour license is emailed to the same address used at checkout, with the installer download and license attachment included.
4. Runtime requirementsCore and Pro can activate locally. Business requires an admin-configured NAS or network share before backup protection is allowed to run.
