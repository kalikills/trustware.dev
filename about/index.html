About Trustware Technologies

A software company built around operational trust.

Trustware Technologies builds software for environments where ambiguity creates risk. We care about explicit rules, enforceable boundaries, and tools that make operational intent visible instead of implied.

What Trustware is

Trustware is the company identity. DevVault is the first public product, but the company direction is broader than a single application.

What we value

We prefer disciplined systems, clear operational boundaries, and software that can be governed instead of merely used.

Where we are headed

We are building a company around trustworthy operational software, with DevVault serving as the first release in that direction.

Current focus

  • DevVault is the current Trustware product line
  • Its focus is backup integrity, restore confidence, signed licensing, and NAS-governed operation
  • The larger company mission is to ship software that makes authority, control, and operational trust explicit
